Project Details

Details

Chess Empire is a mobile game inspired by Shotgun King. This game set in a deep lore, you play as the black king, fighting against the white enemy pieces. Armed with a shotgun, you’re on your own, taking on the entire enemy army. As you progress, you can upgrade your weapon to make it easier to defeat tougher enemies and complete levels. I’m working as a designer on this project, creating and designing the assets for this game.
