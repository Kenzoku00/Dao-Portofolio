Project Details

Details

A Way Up is a game that challenges players to climb floating objects and navigate through progressively difficult levels to reach the highest point on the map. Inspired by the mechanics of "Only Up," if you fall in the middle of level you will restart over your landing place, there is no checkpoint. I am developing this project as indie developer for my school project, using Unreal Engine for developing the game.
